o What are bylaws and their importance? Bylaws are fundamental rules that
govern the body. Bylaws rank above standing rules and other policies.
Changing the bylaws requires formal amendments.

* Defines duties, powers, and limitations. Defines membership. Should
specify committees. Should specify that Robert’s Rules of Order are
used for processes that are not in the bylaws.

= Template bylaws are provided in the presentation emailed.

o Amending bylaws: should follow procedures outlined in the current bylaws.

" Typically require 2/3 majority vote.

" Requires notice of proposed amendment. It may require a vote ata
specific meeting.

o Bestpractices

= Regular reviews to reflect changes to the charter or any changes going
on in the organization. Make sure that it is not in conflict with local
and state laws. Train members on the bylaws.

o Number of important legislature initiatives are taking place concerning criminal
justice. The Bronx District Attorney Darcel Clark will give her thoughts on the state’s
new bail reform law.
o There is positive reaction from the public while speaking about safety and fairness
without mass incarceration.
o Most misdemeanors and nonviolent felonies, cash bail is no longer permitted.
o Judges must release people charged with those crimes with no cash bail while
waiting for a trial.
o For those charged with the most serious crimes, including almost all violent felonies
bail remains.
o Nonviolent felonies, judges will retain the option to set cash bail.
o Clark's office has been ahead of bail reform.

a Michael Beltzer (Ranked Choice Voting NYC) The introduction of ranked-
choice voting allows a voter to rank candidates running for office in order of
preference. If no one gets a majority, the worst performing candidate is
eliminated and their votes are redistributed. That process would continue until
someone gets more than 50 percent of the vote. In the Bronx voters can vote on
the ballot measure November 5. It will be on the back of the ballot. If it passes,
ranked-choice voting would start in 2021.
